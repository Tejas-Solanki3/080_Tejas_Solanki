Virat Kohli

One of the greatest cricketers of the modern era,
Virat Kohli is known for his fiery passion,
unmatched consistency, and fearless leadership.
A former captain of the Indian cricket team,
Kohli has redefined batting with his hunger for runs and countless
match-winning performances.
Beyond the stats, he’s an inspiration—on and off the field.

Indian Cricketer

🏆 Major Achievements of Virat Kohli

  • Most centuries in ODIs (50) – Surpassing Sachin Tendulkar’s long-standing record
  • ICC Cricketer of the Year – 2017, 2018
  • ICC ODI Player of the Year – 2012, 2017, 2018
  • ICC Test Player of the Year – 2018
  • ICC T20I Player of the Year – 2016
  • Fastest century in ODIs by an Indian (52 balls)
  • Fastest player to score 8,000, 9,000, 10,000, and 11,000 runs in ODIs
